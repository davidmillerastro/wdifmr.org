Open Cluster White Dwarf Initial-Final Mass Relation

This website provides current and archived releases of empirical open-cluster white dwarf initial-final mass relation data, together with documentation, references, and related tools.

Contents

The current release includes white dwarf and host-cluster identifiers, atmospheric classifications, cluster properties, white dwarf masses and cooling ages, inferred progenitor masses, uncertainties, quality information, and relevant references. Full definitions and methodological details are provided in the accompanying README.

Acknowledgement and citation

Users of these data are asked to acknowledge this website (wdifmr.org) and to cite the publications associated with the relevant data release.

For the current release, please cite: David R. Miller et al., The White Dwarf Initial-Final Mass Relation from Open Clusters in Gaia DR3, The Astrophysical Journal.
