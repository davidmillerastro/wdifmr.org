About this resource

Purpose

WDIFMR.org is intended to provide a stable and accessible location for empirical white dwarf initial–final mass relation measurements.

Future releases will add updated samples, Python implementations of commonly used IFMRs, plotting tools, and other resources useful for stellar-evolution research.
