Open Cluster White Dwarf Initial-Final Mass Relation

Welcome to wdifmr.org, a resource for researchers using the open-cluster white dwarf initial-final mass relation. The site provides downloadable IFMR data releases, documentation, figures, and references, and will expand to include tools that support the use and implementation of the IFMR.

IFMR tables will be updated as new peer-reviewed results become available. Corrections and suggestions for new features are welcome at drmiller@phas.ubc.ca.
